About
Punishing: Gray Raven (“战双帕弥什”, P:GR) is an online, free-to-play, 3D action role-playing mobile game developed and published by Kuro Game (库洛游戏) (广州库洛游戏, KuroGames inc., Guangzhou) based on the game engine Unity3D. The game is the second production of Kuro Game's "Twintails in Battlefield" (“战场双马尾”) franchise, and was first released in China on December 5, 2019 for Android and iOS. The global version of the game was released on July 16, 2021. The story is set in a post-apocalyptic world in which mankind is almost extinct. Earth has been conquered by a robotic army—the Corrupted—twisted and warped by a biomechanical virus known as the Punishing Virus. The last survivors have fled into orbit, aboard the space station Babylonia. As commandant of the Gray Raven unit, you are tasked with assembling the greatest cyborg soldiers the world has known in order to push back the Corrupted, reclaim the Earth, and unravel the truths behind the Punishing Virus.Punishing: Gray Raven has released in five different regions so far—China, Taiwan, Japan, Korea, and Global (North America, Europe, Asia-Pacific servers)—and features famous voice actors and actresses speaking Chinese (Mandarin), Chinese (Cantonese), and Japanese. The game currently has multiple clients that can be downloaded, supported on iOS, Android, and emulators such as BlueStacks and MuMu.
Character Types
Constructs
Constructs are designed for the majority of the game's content and possess default class ratings of B-Rank, A-Rank, and S-Rank. Constructs have three predesignated classes: Attacker (dealing damage), Tank (weakening enemies), and Support (healing and buffing allies). They cannot be deployed in the same team as Compositers.
Transcendants
All known Uniquants possess a default class rating of S-Rank. Unlike Constructs, Uniquant only possess one predesignated role: Pioneer (also known as Vanguard). Uniquant can be deployed with Constructs of the cost for their ATK stat, 1 construct will give them -30% ATK decrease while having 2 construct will give them -60% ATK decrease.
