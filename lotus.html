Lucia-Lotus
Biography
After the first Lucia had been infected by the Punishing Virus and found out the truth about Luna, the original Lucia decides to join with Luna after knowing Nikola lied about her death. Nikola decided to create a clone of her with the original memories but locked her memories for finding out the truth of her intention to join the Ascendants. Lucia is now the Captain of the Gray Raven Team alongside her commander and her team members Lee and Liv.
